My Journey

How AI became my biggest weapon

// 01

The Realization

Most people use AI like a search engine — type something vague, get something generic. I realized early that the quality of what you get out is entirely determined by what you put in. That's when I started treating prompt engineering like a real craft, not an afterthought.

// 02

Going Deeper

I started studying how these models actually think — chain-of-thought reasoning, role assignment, context windows, temperature settings. I learned to give AI systems a persona, a goal, constraints, examples, and output format all in one prompt. The difference in results was night and day.

// 03

Building with AI

Today I build entire products around AI. Not just calling an API — but designing the full intelligence layer. System prompts, conversation memory, fallback logic, output parsing. I treat AI like a team member I need to onboard properly, and that mindset changes everything.
